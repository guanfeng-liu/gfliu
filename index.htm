Dr. Guanfeng Liu is a Senior Lecturer in School of Computing , Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia. Guanfeng got his PhD in computer science from Macquarie University in 2013. His research interests include graph database, trust management, and social network mining.


Research Interests and Projects
  • Knowledge Graph Search and Mining
    • Abstract: Constructing and searching knowledge graphs efficiently and effectively with graph databased management and graph mining techniques
    • Research Skills: Neutral Language Processing, Graph Database, and Graph Data Mining
  • Social Network Based Recommendation
    • Abstract: Using social contexts, like social relationships and social positions to build up comprehensive profiles for recommendations.
    • Research Skills: Data Mining, and Machine Learning
  • Context-Aware Trust Management in Online Social Networks
    • Abstract: Devising dynamic trust evaluation and trust evolution models with considering the effects of different contexts. The aim is to build up a trustworthy cyber-physical environment.
    • Research Skills: Database, Graph Learning, and Algorithms
