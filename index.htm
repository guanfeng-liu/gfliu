Dr. Guanfeng Liu is an Associate Professor in School of Computing, Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia. His research interests include graph database, graph neural network, and graph data mining. He has published more than 100 papers on international journals and conferences including, IEEE Transactionson Knowledge and Data Engineering, IEEE Transactions on Services Computing, IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computational Intelligence, ACM Transactions on the Web, ACM Transactions on Data Science, SIGIR, ICDE, WWW, IJCAI, AAAI and CIKM, etc.


Research Interests and Topics
  • AI-Powered Medical Data Analytics
    • Abstract: This research aims to develop an advanced AI-driven medical data analytics platform that leverages cutting-edge data science and analytics techniques to improve healthcare decision-making, patient outcomes, and operational efficiency. By integrating machine learning, graph data mining, and predictive modeling, the platform will enable efficient extraction, analysis, and visualization of complex medical data.
    • Research Areas: Predictive Modeling, Graph Databases, Graph Data Mining, Machine Learning, Healthcare Data Analytics

  • Trustworthy AI: Ensuring Reliability, Fairness, and Transparency
    • Abstract: This research aims to develop robust methodologies for building trustworthy AI systems that prioritize reliability, fairness, transparency, and security. By integrating techniques such as explainable AI (XAI), bias mitigation, adversarial robustness, and ethical AI frameworks, the research will address critical challenges in ensuring AI models are interpretable, unbiased, and secure across various applications, including healthcare, finance, and cybersecurity.
    • Research Areas: Explainable AI, Fairness in AI, Adversarial Robustness, Ethical AI, Secure Machine Learning, Trustworthy Data Analytics

  • AI-Powered Database Systems
    • Abstract: This research focuses on the development of next-generation AI-powered database systems designed to revolutionize data management, querying, and analysis processes across various domains. By leveraging advancements in AI, including machine learning, deep learning, and automated optimization, these systems will provide intelligent data indexing, adaptive query optimization, and real-time analytics. The goal is to create highly scalable, self-managing database platforms that can handle large-scale, heterogeneous, and dynamic datasets with exceptional efficiency and flexibility.
    • Research Areas: Automated Query Optimization, Intelligent Data Indexing, Machine Learning for Databases, Deep Learning Applications, Distributed Database Systems, AI-Driven Data Security and Privacy
